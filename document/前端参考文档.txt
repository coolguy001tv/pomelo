eg:<img src="holder.js/200x300/sky">

自定义主题包括5个属性：foreground, background, size, font and fontweight
eg:Holder.add_theme("dark", {background:"#000", foreground:"#aaa", size:11, font: "Monaco", fontweight:"normal"})
